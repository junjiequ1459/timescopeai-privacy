TimeScope.ai Privacy Policy

Last updated: [Current Date]

TL;DR: TimeScope.ai tracks your browsing time locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal data.

What Data We Collect

TimeScope.ai collects the following data locally on your device only:

  • Website domains you visit (e.g., "youtube.com")
  • Time spent on each website
  • User activity data (clicks, scrolls) to detect active vs. idle time
  • Video playback status for accurate activity detection

How We Use Your Data

All data is used exclusively to:

  • Calculate your daily productivity score
  • Show your top visited websites
  • Provide time tracking insights
  • Improve your browsing habits

Data Storage and Security

Your data never leaves your computer.

  • All data is stored locally using Chrome's storage API
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or third parties
  • No accounts, logins, or cloud storage required
  • Data is automatically cleared when you uninstall the extension

Data Sharing

We do not share any data. Since your data never leaves your device, there is nothing to share.

Third-Party Services

TimeScope.ai does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or data collection services.

Your Rights

You can:

  • View all your data within the extension interface
  • Clear your data by uninstalling the extension
  • Use the extension without creating any accounts

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
